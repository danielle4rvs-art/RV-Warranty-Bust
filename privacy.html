Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

RV Warranty Bust ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you use our tool or purchase the appeal kit, we collect the information you provide directly: name, email address, and details about your warranty claim (denial reason, warranty company, state, denial date). Payment is processed securely through Stripe — we do not store your card details.

How We Use It

We use your information to generate your customized appeal letter, deliver your purchase, and send you relevant updates about your order. We may use your email to send occasional product updates, which you can unsubscribe from at any time.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to operate (such as Stripe for payment processing). If you use our free Lemon Law Check or Class Action Check tools and choose to submit your contact information, we will share that information with one matched attorney or law firm partner so they can contact you about your case. We do not send your information to multiple firms, and we do not sell or share your information for any other purpose.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us through the contact information listed on our site.
